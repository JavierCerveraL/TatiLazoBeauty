Refills
  • Refill services receive a complimentary lash bath (highly suggested).
  • Make sure you book the correct service. Example; If you book a 45 min touch up then that is all the time you are going to get.
  • Clients who show up to their refill appointment with poor retention will have the following options;
    1. Pay for a fullset (only if there's enough time).
    2. Pay the refill price but only get 50% - 60% of your lashes filled.
    3. If neither of these work for you, you can cancel your appointment and rebook another time (last minute cancellation fee is required).
  • It is the clients responsibility to let me know ahead of time if they're having any problems with their lashes. Clients will need to provide good quality pictures and videos of their eyes.
  • You must have 70% - 80% of lash extensions left for a one week (4-7 days) touch up refill.
  • You must have no less than 40% - 50% of your lash extensions left for a two week (8-14 days) refill.
  • You must have no less than 40% - 50% of your lash extensions left for a three week (15-21 days) refill.
  • If you notice poor retention 1-3 days after getting your lashes done, you will need to provide good quality pictures/videos no more than 3 days after for me to accept a complimentary OR $20 touch up. The touch up must be done 1-5 days after the initial appointment (with the same amount of lashes).

    • *Note* I personally need my lashes filled every 10-14 days due to having oily skin, fast lash shedding, humidity, etc. Everybody is different and your lashes MAY NOT last the full three weeks.

Weak/ Damaged / Poor Natural Lashes
  • Unfortunately, I will not work on clients (especially those who want long/dramatic lashes) if they have weak/damaged/poor natural lashes
  • Retention is different for everyone. Here are some of the reasons you may experience poor retention; stress, aging (hair loss & weak lashes), wearing masks/face shields, steam/saunas, heat, new medications, oily skin, picking/pulling, not cleansing everyday properly, not using a proper lash wash, skin care, side sleepers ETC.

    • ** Please book a 15 minute free consultation on my website if you are not sure if your natural lashes are in good condition.**
